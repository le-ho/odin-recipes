Bolognese

description

Perfect for lasagne or tagliatelle dish

Ingridients

  • 45 ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 25g butter
  • 1 x onion, finely chopped
  • 1 x celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 x carrot, finely chopped
  • 150g pancetta, cubed
  • 200g minced beef
  • 200g minced pork
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 2 x tablespoons double-concentrated tomato puree
  • 200 ml stock

Steps

  1. Heat the extra virgin olive oil and butter in a pan
  2. dd the onion, celery, carrot and pancetta and sweat
  3. Add the meat and brown all over.
  4. Increase the heat, add the wine and allow to evaporate
  5. Dilute the tomato puree in a little of the stock and stir into the meat.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover with a lid and cook on a gentle heat for 2 hours, checking and adding a little extra stock from time to time to avoid the sauce from drying out.
