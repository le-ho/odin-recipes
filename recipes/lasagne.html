Lasagne

Description

What a beautiful dish

Everybody should make it once a week

Ingridients

  • 40g butter
  • 40g plain flour
  • 500ml milk
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 100g grated parmesan cheese
  • About 8 x dried egg lasagne sheets

Steps

  1. First make the Bolognese ragu see recipe
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees centigrade.
  3. Make white sauce
  4. Line an ovenproof dish with a little of the Bolognese sauce,
  5. bake it
